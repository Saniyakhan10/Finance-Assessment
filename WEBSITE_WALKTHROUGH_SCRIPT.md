# ShareMarket Application - Walkthrough Script

## 1. Introduction
"Welcome to **ShareMarket**, your all-in-one financial dashboard designed for the modern investor. This platform brings real-time stock data, mutual fund insights, and economic indicators together in a premium, dark-themed interface built with Next.js."

## 2. Hero Section
**Visual:** Large, bold typography with a "Start Investing Free" CTA.
**Script:** "We start with the **Hero Section**. It instantly grabs attention with a clean, modern aesthetic. The animated 'India's Most Trusted Platform' badge builds trust, while key statistics like '10M+ Active Users' and '₹50K Cr AUM' showcase the scale of the platform."

## 3. Intraday Widget (Real-Time Chart)
**Visual:** A large, interactive area chart showing stock price movement (default: Reliance).
**Script:** "Scrolling down, we have the **Intraday Widget**. This component visualizes real-time stock performance. It uses a dynamic area chart to show price trends for key stocks like Reliance, giving traders an instant snapshot of the day's volatility."

## 4. Stocks Section (Top Gainers & Losers)
**Visual:** A responsive grid of cards showing stock tickers, prices, and green/red percentage changes.
**Script:** "Next is the **Stocks Section**. Here, we highlight the market's Top Gainers and Losers. Each card displays the stock symbol, current price, and percentage change, colour-coded to instantly indicate positive or negative movement."

## 5. Mutual Funds Section
**Visual:** A search bar and featured fund cards (HDFC, ICICI, SBI) with 'Invest Now' buttons.
**Script:** "For long-term investors, we have the **Mutual Funds Section**.
*   **Search Functionality:** You can search for any mutual fund scheme in real-time.
*   **Live Data:** The featured cards display vital info like 1-Year Returns and Minimum SIP amounts, fetched directly from live market APIs."

## 6. SIP Calculator (New Feature)
**Visual:** A dedicated black section with sliders for Investment, Rate, and Time, plus a projection chart.
**Script:** "Right below that is our newly integrated **SIP Calculator**.
*   **Interactive Tools:** Users can adjust their monthly investment, expected return, and time period using smooth sliders.
*   **Real-World Projections:** What sets this apart is the ability to select a *real* mutual fund. The calculator then fetches that fund's actual historical return rate to give you a data-backed wealth projection, visualized by a growth chart."

## 7. Commodities Section (Gold & Silver)
**Visual:** Two charts side-by-side for Gold and Silver spot prices.
**Script:** "We also track **Global Commodities**. This section provides live spot prices and historical trend charts for Gold and Silver, essential for diversifying investment portfolios."

## 8. Retail Sales & Economic Data
**Visual:** A bar chart showing retail sales trends.
**Script:** "To help you understand the broader economy, the **Retail Sales Section** provides key macroeconomic indicators, visualizing consumer spending trends over time."

## 9. Stock Events & News
**Visual:** A timeline of upcoming events (Earnings, IPOs) and a grid of news articles.
**Script:** "Stay ahead of the market with **Stock Events**, ensuring you never miss an earnings call or IPO. The **News Section** aggregates the latest financial headlines with sentiment analysis (Positive/Negative tags) to help you gauge market mood."

## 10. Conclusion
**Script:** "In summary, ShareMarket is a robust, data-driven application that empowers users with the tools they need—from researching individual stocks and funds to planning their financial future with accurate calculators."
